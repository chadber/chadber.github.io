My Room is My Place Where I Call Home

My room is my home. A place where I can write my thoughts, think about my goals, create and dream. I believe a home is a place where you can just be, to be free. A place that is comfortable, a place to relax.

In the mornings I wake up, look out my window and brew my coffee. My mind wonders as I smell the fresh Sumatra blend aroma fill my room. My eyes focus as I put my glasses on. Broken images and thoughts of my next character fill my mind. A sudden flash of how I should draw my next design inspires me.
